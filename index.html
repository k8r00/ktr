About

I believe strong institutions improve lives by showing smoother paths forward, and thus growing time for what brings joy to the soul.

Whether through manufacturing, technology, education, or community initiatives, I am interested in creating systems that outlast the people who build them.

Today, I serve as a director in a Philippine manufacturing company, pursuing projects in operations, digital systems, renewable energy, electric mobility, and scientific education.

Long-term, my vision is to help create places where people can continue learning, building, and contributing throughout their lives.
